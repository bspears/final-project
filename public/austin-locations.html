Barton Springs Pool

Landmark, Outdoor

Take a dip to escape the summer heat in this expansive natural limestone pool.

Barton Springs Pool is situated at the grounds of Zilker Park. It is a fantastic place to cool off in the summer, with a 68 degree average water temperature year round. Over the years, people have come to this 1,000 foot long natural limestone pool, fed by several underground springs, for everything from fishing and swimming to baptisms and beauty pageants.

Address: 2201 Barton Springs Rd, Zilker Park, Austin,TX 78704

Alamo Drafthouse

Venue, Restaurant

Come sit for awhile and enjoy the amenities at this iconic local theatre.

The Alamo Drafthouse is a well-known movie theater which offers food and drink service inside while patrons enjoy their movies. This theater also hosts different types of interactive events, along with showings of all-time favorite classics and first run movies.

Address: Multiple locations in Austin

Umlauf Sculpture Garden and Museum

Landmark, Outdoor

Wander through the gardens while viewing the works of sculptor Charles Umlauf, including a rotating gallery featuring some of his more obscure works.

The Umlauf museum and garden is dedicated to the works of American sculptor Charles Umlauf. The garden holds over 40 sculptures framed by nature. Inside the gallery, rotating exhibits of Umlauf's more obscure pieces and other similar artists are on display. There are everyday activities for children and regular workshops and lectures for art-lovers of all ages.

Address: 605 Robert E. Lee Road Austin, Texas 78704
