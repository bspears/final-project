Central Park

Landmark, Outdoor

Take time to explore New York’s expansive 843 acre Central Park, located in the heart of Manhattan.

Spanning 843 acres in the heart of Manhattan, Central Park is one of the world's greatest urban oases, encompassing a diverse landscape of rolling fields, walking trails and tranquil bodies of water—all sculpted by human hands. It is an absolute must-see for any travelers coming through the Big Apple.

59th to 110th St., from Central Park West to 5th Ave., New York City, NY 10022

Apollo Theater

Venue

Harlems Apollo Theatre has been the home for the start of many legendary Entertainers as well as being a historic icon within the borough.

Harlem’s famous Apollo Theater is important not only because it honors and promotes the contributions of black American performers, but also because it is a living example of progress. Visit the Apollo for an Amateur Night, which started the careers of so many legends—including Ella Fitzgerald—or take a historical tour of the building.

253 W. 125th St. Manhattan, NY 10027

Bronx Zoo

Outdoor

For the animal lover in all of us, the Bronx Zoo offers an experience like none other.

The Bronx Zoo is the largest urban wildlife preserve in the United States, home to several authentically re-created habitats that house more than 5,000 animals representing more than 600 species. Take your whole family to this fantastic zoo and you won’t be disappointed.

2300 Southern Boulevard, Bronx, NY 10460
